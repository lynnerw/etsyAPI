Balboa Park

Where is Balboa Park? San Diego, California

What is Balboa Park?

  • 1,200 acres (490 hectares)
  • 13 museums
  • 16 gardens and an indoor botanical garden

Not enough?

Balboa Park also has:

  • The world famous San Diego Zoo
  • An art institute
  • A history center
  • Three cultural centers
  • An amphitheater, a Shakespearean theater, and a pupper theater
  • A stadium and a pavilion
  • Like sports? It has a golf courses, ball fields, tennis courts, archery ranges and a swimming pool.

Over 12 million people visit the Balboa Park every year; why not you?
